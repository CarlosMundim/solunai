HOW IT WORKS

2-6 Week Placement Process

Our streamlined process gets you the right talent fast

1

Requirements Analysis

We meet to understand your needs, company culture, and specific role requirements. Define technical skills, soft skills, and cultural fit criteria.

2

Global Talent Search

Our global network spans Vietnam, Indonesia, China, and beyond. We tap into pre-vetted talent pools and conduct targeted searches across multiple countries.

3

AI-Powered Screening

aiKODA cultural intelligence and iWORKZ matching algorithms screen candidates. We present only the top 3-5 candidates that meet your criteria.

4

Interviews & Selection

You interview shortlisted candidates via video calls. We facilitate technical assessments, cultural fit interviews, and reference checks.

5

Onboarding & Integration

Pre-boarding training begins immediately. We handle all HR paperwork, compliance, and setup. Your new colleague starts productive from day one.

6

Ongoing Success

Continuous support for both employer and employee. Regular check-ins, performance monitoring, and issue resolution to ensure long-term success.
