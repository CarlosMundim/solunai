Our Story

Solunai was founded on a simple but powerful insight: the future belongs to those who can bridge cultures, harness AI responsibly, and build sustainably.

Born in Japan, we witnessed firsthand the challenges of cultural integration in global business. We saw talented professionals struggling to adapt, companies missing opportunities due to cultural misalignment, and infrastructure that couldn't keep pace with AI's energy demands.

So we built aiKODA - a platform that doesn't just match skills, but understands the 47 dimensions of cultural intelligence needed for Japanese business success. We partnered with WorldEmp to bring global talent to Japan with unprecedented speed and cultural fit. And we're pioneering zero-carbon datacenter infrastructure to power the AI revolution sustainably.

Today, Solunai operates across three integrated divisions - HPC Lab (28 AI solutions), WorldEmp Japan (global talent), and Datacenter Infrastructure (zero-carbon power) - all united by a common mission: build from Japan, serve the world.
